When did you start running?

That's a question I get a lot and it's one I don't know how to answer.
My running journey has been very long and it's had many ups and downs.
So here's the full story:

Elementary School
2001–2007
The fast kid
Soccer, wrestling, baseball, flag football; I played a lot of sports and was "the fast kid"
Middle School
2008–2010
The short kid
I started running competitively in middle school. I wasn't very good, puberty was not kind to me. I did cross country + track
High School
2011
Freshman year
I continued with cross country and track (distance), 21:48 5k, 5:45 mile
2012
Sophomore year
I quit running after my second year of cross country. 20:48 5k. Decided to focus exclusively on wrestling
2012–2014
Wrestling years
I didn't run
College
2015–2018
College years
I only ran casually and was very inconsistent, My 5k time dropped to 23m I lost a lot of fitness
Post-College
2018
First adult race
Signed up for a 15k and started training again
2019
Marathon debut
Ran the Chicago Marathon with a training group, 3h48m
2020–2021
COVID & injuries
Stopped running during COVID due to injury & laziness, this was the most out of shape I've ever been
2021
The comeback
Started running again. Raced a proper half-marathon after 6 months of nonstop training, 1h39m
2022
Consistency
Started running consistently with coworkers. Completed the 9+1 program for the first time. First year running more than 1,000 miles
2023
Marathon return
Return to the marathon after 3.5 years, 3h29m. Joined my first run club outside of work
2024
Discovering Ultras
First ultramarathon: the Manhattan Perimeter. 3 more ultras and 2 marathons in the same year
2025
The run club era
First 100-mile race. Joined many more run clubs to help with training. Started posting to TikTok
2026
Getting experimental
who knows what's next?
