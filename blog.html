When did you start running?

That's a question I get a lot and it's one I don't know how to answer. My running journey has been very long and it's had many ups and downs. So here's the full story:

Elementary School
2001–2007
All The Sports
  • Played a lot of sports
  • Soccer, wrestling, baseball, flag football, swimming
  • Wrestling was my only good sport
Middle School
2008–2010
The Short Kid
  • Started running competitively in middle school
  • Cross Country + Track (some sprinting, some field, some distance)
  • Wasn't very good
  • Everyone else was bigger and taller
High School
2011
The Slow Kid (Freshman)
  • Continued with Cross Country + Track (distance)
  • My team was very good
  • I was one of the slowest freshmen
  • 21:48 5k
  • 5:45 mile
2012
Not Good Enough (Sophomore)
  • Quit running after my second year of Cross Country
  • 20:48 5k
  • The incoming freshmen were already faster than me
  • They were also bigger and taller than me
  • I was never going to make Varsity
  • Decided to focus exclusively on wrestling
2012–2014
The Wrestler
  • Didn't run seriously
  • Got much better at wrestling
College
2015–2018
Nerd Years
  • Only ran casually
  • Was very inconsistent
  • Ran a 5k turkey trot in 24m
Post-College
2018
Finding a Hobby
  • Signed up for my first adult race (Hot Chocolate 15k)
  • Started tracking my runs on Strava
2019
Marathon Debut
  • Got into the Chicago Marathon lottery
  • Joined a marathon training group
  • Ran my first marathon
  • 3h48m
  • It was the worst race experience of my life
2020–2021
COVID & Injuries
  • Got a running injury before COVID hit
  • Lost motivation after recovering
  • Stopped working out for almost 2 years
  • Fell into the worst shape of my life
2021
The Comeback
  • Started running again when a friend asked to do a half-marathon
  • Trained hard for 6 months
  • It was the best race of my life
  • 1h39m half-marathon
  • I found my motivation again
2022
Consistency
  • Started running consistently with coworkers
  • They helped to keep me accountable
  • Did 9+1 for the first time
  • It was my first year running more than 1,000 miles
2023
Marathon Return
  • I was really scared to race a marathon again after 3.5 years
  • Returned with a 3h33m in Cincinatti
  • Then joined my first real run club
  • and ran a 3h29m in New York
2024
Discovering Ultras
  • Ran my first solo ultra: The Manhattan Perimeter
  • Had so much fun that I ran 3 more ultras and 2 marathons that year
2025
The Run Club Era
  • Joined a lot more run clubs
  • Ran my first 100-mile ultra
  • Started posting to TikTok
2026
Getting Experimental
  • Started posting to Instagram
  • ‎
  • ... what's next?
